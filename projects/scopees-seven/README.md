# Scopee's Seven — Rules

## Objective

Be the first player to score **200 points**. Score points by collecting Number cards each round. If you draw a duplicate number, you bust and score nothing. If you collect all 7 unique numbers, you trigger a **Majestic Flip** — the round ends immediately and you earn a +15 bonus.

---

## The Deck (94 cards)

- **Number cards (0–12):** The higher the value, the more copies in the deck. The 0 is a single copy — rare, but it counts toward the 7-card goal.
- **Modifier cards:** x2, +2, +4, +6, +8, +10
- **Action cards:** Second Chance, Freeze, Flip Three

---

## Setup

1. Shuffle the deck thoroughly.
2. Choose a Dealer.
3. Have pen and paper ready to track scores (or use the app).

---

## Each Round

The Dealer deals one card face-up to each player, including themselves. If an Action card comes up during the deal, resolve it immediately before continuing. Not every player will have a Number card after the deal — that's normal.

Once everyone has been dealt a card, the player to the Dealer's left takes the first turn and play proceeds clockwise.

On your turn, choose:
- **Hit** — draw a card from the deck
- **Stay** — bank your points and exit the round

---

## Card Types

### Number Cards (0–12)

Place them face-up in front of you. They make up your score at the end of the round.

- Draw a **duplicate** of a number you already have → **Bust**: discard all your cards, score 0 this round, and sit out until the next round.
- Collect **7 unique Number cards** → **Majestic Flip**: the round ends immediately for everyone, and you score all your points plus a **+15 bonus**.

The 0 is a valid Number card — it contributes 0 to your score but brings you one step closer to a Majestic Flip.

### Modifier Cards

Modifier cards do **not** cause a bust. You can Stay with only Modifier cards and no Number cards (your score would be 0, but you exit safely).

| Card | Effect |
|------|--------|
| x2   | Doubles your Number card total (applied before +N cards) |
| +2 / +4 / +6 / +8 / +10 | Adds the shown amount to your total |

### Action Cards

Action cards can be played on **any active player**, including yourself. You cannot hold them — they must be resolved immediately.

**Second Chance**
Protects against one bust. If you draw a duplicate while holding Second Chance, discard the duplicate instead of busting — the Second Chance card is then also discarded. A player can only hold one at a time; if you're dealt a second, give it to another active player who doesn't already have one. Second Chance does **not** protect against a Freeze played by an opponent. All Second Chance cards are discarded at the end of each round, even if never used.

**Freeze**
The target player must Stay immediately, banking whatever points they currently have.

**Flip Three**
The target player must immediately draw 3 cards one at a time, resolving each before drawing the next. Those 3 cards can themselves be Action cards, which may be played on any active player.

> **Note:** When you are the last active player, you must play Action cards on yourself.

---

## End of a Round

The round ends when either:
1. All players have busted or stayed, **or**
2. A player achieves a **Majestic Flip** (7 unique Number cards)

---

## Scoring

Calculate in this order:

1. Sum the values of your Number cards
2. If you have the **x2** Modifier, double the sum
3. Add any **+N** Modifier cards
4. If you triggered the **Majestic Flip**, add **+15**

Busted players score **0**.

> The x2 card only multiplies Number cards — not the +15 Majestic Flip bonus or +N Modifier cards.

---

## Starting the Next Round

- Set all cards to the side without shuffling them back in yet.
- The player to the left of the current Dealer becomes the new Dealer.
- If the deck runs out mid-round, shuffle the discarded cards to form a new deck.

---

## End of the Game

After a round ends, if at least one player has **200 or more points**, the player with the **most points wins**. If two or more players are tied at 200+, keep playing until one player is the sole leader.

---

## Quick Reference

| Situation | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Draw a duplicate number | Bust — score 0, exit round |
| Collect 7 unique numbers | Majestic Flip — +15 bonus, round ends |
| Stay with no cards | Score 0, exit safely |
| x2 Modifier | Doubles Number card sum only |
| Freeze | Target must Stay immediately |
| Flip Three | Target draws 3 cards immediately |
| Second Chance | Blocks one bust from a duplicate number |
| Reach 200+ points (sole leader) | Win the game |
