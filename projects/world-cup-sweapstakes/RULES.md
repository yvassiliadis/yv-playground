# World Cup 2026 Sweepstakes Rules

## Team Assignment

48 teams are divided into 4 tiers based on betting odds at the time of the draw:

| Tier | Label | Teams |
|------|-------|-------|
| 1 | Favorites | Spain, France, England, Portugal, Brazil, Argentina, Germany, Netherlands |
| 2 | Strong | Norway, Belgium, Colombia, Morocco, Mexico, Japan, Switzerland, United States, Uruguay, Ecuador, Türkiye, Croatia, Senegal |
| 3 | Mid-tier | Austria, Sweden, Ivory Coast, Scotland, Canada, Czechia, Paraguay, South Korea, Australia, Algeria, Egypt, Bosnia & Herzegovina, Ghana |
| 4 | Long-shots | Tunisia, Iran, South Africa, DR Congo, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Cape Verde, Iraq, Panama, Uzbekistan, New Zealand, Jordan, Curaçao, Haiti |

Each participant is randomly assigned **one team per tier** (4 teams total). Some teams are shared between participants — portfolios of 4 teams are unique.

## Scoring

### Group Stage (per match)
| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Win | 3 pts |
| Draw | 1 pt |
| Loss | 0 pts |

### Knockout Progression (cumulative)
| Round reached | Points |
|---------------|--------|
| Round of 32 | +2 pts |
| Round of 16 | +3 pts |
| Quarter-final | +5 pts |
| Semi-final | +8 pts |
| Final | +12 pts |
| **Win World Cup** | **+20 pts** |

### 3rd Place Match
| Result | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Win 3rd place | +3 pts |
| Lose 3rd place | +0 pts |

### Bonus
| Bonus | Points |
|-------|--------|
| Your team's player wins the Golden Boot | +5 pts |

### Example Scenarios

**Dream run** — You have Spain. They win all 3 group games, go all the way, win the final, and their striker wins the Golden Boot.
3 group wins (9) + R32 (2) + R16 (3) + QF (5) + SF (8) + Final (12) + WC win (20) + Golden Boot (5) = **64 pts from Spain alone**

**Solid exit** — You have Colombia. They go 2W 1L in the group stage, beat one team in the Round of 32, then lose in the Round of 16.
2 group wins (6) + R32 (2) + R16 (3) = **11 pts from Colombia**

**Tie-breaker** — Jamie and Sam finish level on points. Jamie's 4 teams scored 23 goals across the tournament; Sam's scored 19. Jamie wins on tie-breaker #1.

---

## Tie-breakers

If participants finish level on total points, ties are broken in this order:

1. Total goals scored by all 4 assigned teams across the tournament
2. Total knockout-stage points earned by all 4 assigned teams
3. Total group-stage points earned by all 4 assigned teams
4. Fewest goals conceded by all 4 assigned teams
5. Highest score from the participant's assigned Tier 4 team
6. Highest score from the participant's assigned Tier 3 team
7. Fine, have it your way, and keep it a tie!

---

## Overall Winner

Highest total points across all 4 assigned teams wins.
