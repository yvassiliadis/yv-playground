The Rules

Your four teams earn points across the whole tournament. Group-stage results, knockout progression, a third-place play-off, and a Golden Boot bonus all stack up. Highest combined total wins.

Scoring

Win+3 pts

// The four pots

Tiers

Every participant drew one team from each tier — one favorite, one strong side, one mid-tier outfit, and one long-shot. Here's how the 48 nations were grouped.

Tiers were defined by clustering betting odds from major bookmakers using k-means on a log scale. Natural gaps in the odds distribution determined the four groups — so the boundaries reflect where the market itself sees real jumps in a team's chances, not arbitrary cut-offs.
